I am a first year graduate student at the University of Osnabrück under the supervision of Paul Breiding. I am part of the Emmy-Noether Research Group “Numerical and Probabilistic Nonlinear Algebra”. My research interests involve commutative algebra, algebraic geometry and nonlinear algebra.

Before that, I earned my master's degree at Moscow State University under the supervision of Sergey Gaifullin. I also worked as a research assistant in the Laboratory on Algebraic Transformation Groups.
